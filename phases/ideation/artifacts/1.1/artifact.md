## The Mom Test Compliance

These materials follow The Mom Test principles:

âœ… **Focus on past behavior, not hypotheticals**
- Questions ask about recent, specific experiences
- "Tell me about the last time..." not "Would you..."

âœ… **Listen more than you talk**
- Script designed for 70% listening, 30% asking
- Probing questions to go deeper
- No pitching or convincing

âœ… **Don't ask if they like your idea**

- Listen for genuine resonance, not politeness
- Focus on their problems first

âœ… **Dig into specifics and emotions**

## Support and Troubleshooting

### If Response Rates Are Low
- Increase incentive amount
- Shorten outreach messages
- Leverage more personal network connections
- Try different channels (WhatsApp groups, community forums)

### If Not Getting Good Data
- Review The Mom Test principles
- Are you asking about past behavior?
- Are you listening enough?
- Are you digging into specifics?
- Practice with a colleague first

### If Interviews Are Taking Too Long
- Focus on highest-priority questions
- Cut less critical probing questions
- Aim for 30 minutes total (not 45)
- Keep Solution section concise

### If Results Are Unclear
- Conduct 2-3 additional interviews
- Focus on personas with strongest signals
- Dig deeper into specific KPIs that are borderline
- Look for pattern consistency
