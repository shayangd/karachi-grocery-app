## SAM Estimation

### Method 1: Top-Down from TAM

**Pakistan Grocery Delivery Market Data**:
- **Pakistan Food Delivery Market**: $2.4B (2024, per Tech in Asia)
- **Pakistan E-Commerce Market**: $8B (2024, per Instagram report)
- **Karachi Population**: 18-20M (metro area)
- **Grocery Shoppers in Karachi**: 10-12M (per venture idea statement)

**TAM Calculation**:
```
TAM (Pakistan Online Grocery) = Pakistan E-Commerce × Grocery %
TAM = $8B × 15% (grocery as % of e-commerce)
TAM = $1.2B (Pakistan online grocery market estimate)
```

**SAM Filters**:
- **Geographic Filter**: Karachi only = 55% of Pakistan's e-commerce activity
- **Segment Filter**: Urban, smartphone users with delivery access = 70%
- **Use-Case Filter**: Frequent grocery shoppers (3-5x/week) = 60%

**SAM Calculation**:
```
SAM = TAM × Geographic % × Segment % × Use-Case %
SAM = $1.2B × 55% × 70% × 60%
SAM = $1.2B × 0.231
SAM = $277M (Karachi addressable grocery delivery market)
```

**Alternative SAM Estimate** (from Pakistan food delivery market):
```
Pakistan Food Delivery = $2.4B (2024)
Grocery/Q-Commerce = 30% of food delivery market (emerging category)
Pakistan Q-Commerce SAM = $2.4B × 30% = $720M
Karachi Share = $720M × 55% = $396M

Conservative SAM (average): ($277M + $396M) / 2 = $336M
```

**SAM Estimate (Top-Down)**: **$280-400M** (Karachi annual addressable market)
