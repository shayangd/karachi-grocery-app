## Executive Summary

This research provides financial and operational data for 8 competitors in Pakistan's grocery delivery/q-commerce market. **Key Finding**: Pakistan-specific financial data (revenue, customer counts, ARPU) is extremely limited due to private company status. We leverage global benchmarks (India q-commerce, US online grocery) and proportional scaling to estimate market size and unit economics.

**Market Size Estimate**: Karachi online grocery delivery SAM = **$40-60M** (2025)
**Market Structure**: Duopoly emerging - Foodpanda (60-70% share) + Krave Mart (15-20%)
**ARPU Estimate**: **$30-50/customer/year** for Pakistan q-commerce
**AOV Estimate**: **$10-15/order**, 3-5 orders/month

## SAM Estimation

### Method 1: Top-Down

**Pakistan E-commerce**: $2.8B (2024)
**Online Grocery**: 15-20% = $504M
**Karachi (18% of Pakistan)**: $90.7M
**Q-commerce Filter (40%)**: **$36M**

### Method 2: India Proportional Scaling

**India Q-commerce**: $6.5B GMV (2024)
**Karachi (1.7% of India pop)**: $110M
**GDP Adjustment (55%)**: **$60M**

### Final SAM Estimate

**$40-60M** (Karachi online grocery delivery/q-commerce, 2025)
