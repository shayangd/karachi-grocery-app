## SOM Calculation Inputs

**Variables for Synthesizer**:

```yaml
# Population Inputs
total_karachi_population: 21,000,000  # 2025 estimate
total_karachi_households: 3,500,000  # 21M ÷ 6 members/household

# ICP Filtering

icp_by_geography_filter: 600,000  # ICP households in Clifton/DHA/Gulshan (57% of income ICP)

# Geographic Concentration
phase_1_launch_areas:
  - Clifton/DHA: 350,000 households
  - Gulshan-e-Iqbal: 250,000 households
  - Total Phase 1: 600,000 households (57% of total ICP)

# Reachability Inputs
digital_reachability: 78%  # % of ICP reachable via mobile app + social media + referral
phase_1_addressable: 468,000  # 600K × 78% reachability

# Realistic Market Share (Grocery Delivery Category)
market_share_year_1: 0.5-1%  # Conservative - new entrant, building trust
market_share_year_3: 3-7%  # Aggressive - if real-time inventory + traffic routing work
market_share_year_5: 8-15%  # Best case - category leader in Karachi

# Competitor Benchmarks
competitor_users_estimate:
  - Daraz grocery: ~150,000-300,000 active users in Karachi (40-45% Pakistan e-commerce market share)
  - Pandamart: ~100,000-200,000 active Karachi users
  - InstaShop: ~50,000-100,000 active Karachi users
  - Total competitive users: ~300,000-600,000 (6-12% of ICP households)
  - Opportunity: 75% of users willing to switch (Recipe 1.2), indicating under-served market
```

### SOM Formula (for synthesizer to apply):

```
# Step 1: Calculate Reachable ICP
Reachable_ICP_Phase_1 = Phase_1_Households × Reachability_%
Reachable_ICP_Phase_1 = 600,000 × 78% = 468,000 households

# Step 2: Apply Market Share %
SOM_Households_Year_1 = Reachable_ICP × Market_Share_Year_1
SOM_Households_Year_1 = 468,000 × 0.75% (midpoint) = 3,510 households

SOM_Households_Year_3 = 468,000 × 5% (midpoint) = 23,400 households

SOM_Households_Year_5 = 468,000 × 11.5% (midpoint) = 53,820 households

# Step 3: Calculate Order Frequency
Orders_Per_Household_Per_Month = 12-16 orders/month (3-4x per week, per Recipe 1.2)
Annual_Orders_Per_Household = 144-192 orders/year (using 12/month conservative)

# Step 4: Calculate SOM Revenue
ARPU_Per_Order = PKR 1,500-2,000 (average grocery basket, validated in Recipe 1.2: PKR 4,525/week)
Revenue_Per_Household_Per_Year = 144 orders × PKR 1,750 (midpoint) = PKR 252,000

SOM_Revenue_Year_1 = 3,510 households × PKR 252,000 = PKR 884.5M (~USD $3.1M)
SOM_Revenue_Year_3 = 23,400 households × PKR 252,000 = PKR 5.90B (~USD $20.9M)
SOM_Revenue_Year_5 = 53,820 households × PKR 252,000 = PKR 13.56B (~USD $48.1M)
```
