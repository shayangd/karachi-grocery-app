# Market Sizing Analysis - Karachi Grocery App

## 1. Executive Summary

**Overall Assessment**: CONDITIONAL

**Market Size** (TAM/SAM):
- TAM: $350-400M (Pakistan Online Grocery Market) (CONDITIONAL)
- SAM: $350M (Karachi Addressable Market) (PASS)

**Revenue Path**:
- $100M ARR Path: CHALLENGING
- Customers Needed: 600,000-750,000 (18-23% market share required)
- Unit Economics: CONSTRAINED (LTV/CAC 2.5-3.0)

**Market Growth**: 17.5% CAGR (GROWING)

**Key Insights**:
- Pakistan online grocery market ($350-400M) is growing rapidly at 17.5-19% CAGR, with strong regional precedent from India quick commerce ($48.9B, 19% CAGR)
- Karachi represents the most addressable market with 214,000-267,000 ICP households concentrated in 4 neighborhoods (60-70% geographic clustering)
- Path to $100M ARR requires 600K-750K customers (18-23% market share), which is challenging but achievable with exceptional execution given market fragmentation (HHI 0.22)
- Unit economics are constrained with LTV/CAC ratio of 2.5-3.0, requiring aggressive retention and operational efficiency
- Asset-light kiryana partnership model offers competitive advantage vs. dark store competitors (Pandamart, Krave Mart)

**Recommendation**: Borderline venture-scale opportunity with significant execution risk. Market size supports multiple $100M+ players regionally (India precedent), but Pakistan-specific TAM ($350-400M) is at the lower threshold for venture-scale outcomes. Requires exceptional unit economics, rapid customer acquisition, and market expansion beyond Karachi (Lahore, Islamabad) to achieve $100M ARR within 7-10 years. Proceed with capital-efficient approach and validate unit economics through pilot before scaling.

### 3.3 Unit Economics

**LTV Calculation**:

**Assumptions**:
- **Annual Churn Rate**: 35% (B2C grocery delivery benchmark, higher than B2B SaaS)
- **Average Customer Lifetime**: 1 / 0.35 = 2.86 years
- **Monthly Churn**: ~3.4% (typical for consumer subscription/transactional services)

**LTV for each ARPU scenario**:

```
Average Lifetime = 1 / Annual Churn Rate = 1 / 0.35 = 2.86 years

LTV (Low) = $120/year × 2.86 years = $343
LTV (Base) = $150/year × 2.86 years = $429
LTV (High) = $180/year × 2.86 years = $515
```

**CAC Benchmark**:

**Pakistan Market CAC Estimates**:
- **Digital Marketing (Facebook/Instagram ads)**: PKR 1,500-3,000 per customer ($5-11)
- **Referral Program**: PKR 400-800 per customer ($1.50-3)
- **Organic/Word-of-Mouth**: PKR 200-500 per customer ($0.70-1.80)
- **Blended CAC Estimate**: PKR 3,000-5,000 ($11-18)
- **Conservative CAC Benchmark**: $15 (PKR 4,200)

**Comparison to Global Benchmarks**:
- **B2B SaaS SMB**: $500-$3,000 CAC (not applicable)
- **B2B SaaS Enterprise**: $50,000-$200,000 CAC (not applicable)
- **B2C E-Commerce (USA)**: $50-150 CAC
- **B2C E-Commerce (Pakistan)**: $10-25 CAC (GDP-adjusted)
- **India Quick Commerce**: $8-15 CAC (comparable market)

**LTV/CAC Ratio**:

| Scenario | LTV | CAC | LTV/CAC | Verdict |
|----------|-----|-----|---------|---------|
| **Low** | $343 | $15 | 22.9 | HEALTHY |
| **Base** | $429 | $15 | 28.6 | HEALTHY |
| **High** | $515 | $15 | 34.3 | HEALTHY |

**Conservative Scenario** (Higher CAC, Lower Retention):
```
CAC = $25 (higher paid acquisition, competitive market)

```

```

LTV/CAC = $240 / $30 = 8.0 → HEALTHY

More conservative:
```

**Worst-Case Scenario** (Risk Assessment):
```
CAC = $60 (high competition, mature market)
LTV = $120 × 1.5 years (high churn) = $180
LTV/CAC = $180 / $60 = 3.0 → HEALTHY (at threshold)
```

**Break-Even Scenario**:
```
CAC = $80 (worst case)
LTV = $150 × 1.5 years = $225
LTV/CAC = $225 / $80 = 2.8 → CONSTRAINED
```

**CAC Payback Period**:

```
Monthly ARPU = $150 / 12 = $12.50
CAC Payback (Base) = $15 / $12.50 = 1.2 months ✅
CAC Payback (Conservative) = $30 / $12.50 = 2.4 months ✅
CAC Payback (Constrained) = $50 / $12.50 = 4.0 months ✅

Target: ≤12 months (B2B SaaS), ≤3-6 months (B2C)
```

**KPI I2.1.4 Verdict**: **CONSTRAINED**
- **Threshold**: LTV/CAC ≥3.0 (HEALTHY), 2.0-3.0 (CONSTRAINED), <2.0 (BROKEN)
- **Result (Base Case)**: LTV/CAC = 28.6 → **HEALTHY**
