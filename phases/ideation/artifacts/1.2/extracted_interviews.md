## Key Insights

### Top Pain Points (by frequency)
1. Unpredictable stock availability (12/12) - Universal problem
2. Traffic/time waste (10/12) - Especially peak hours
3. Price inconsistency (8/12) - No transparency
4. Delivery delays/cancellations (7/12) - Existing apps fail
5. Rain/weather disruption (5/12) - Seasonal but severe

### Geographic Patterns
- DHA/Clifton: Higher spend, willing to pay premium
- Gulshan/Johar: High pain, price-sensitive
- North areas: Less served by existing apps
- Industrial areas (Korangi): Almost no delivery coverage

### Competitive Landscape Feedback
- Daraz Mart: Slow, out-of-stock common
- PandaMart: Limited coverage, cancelled orders
- Krave Mart (defunct): Was popular, missed in market
- Local Kiryana: Trusted but no visibility into inventory

## Interview Methodology Notes

- All interviews conducted in person or via video call
- 45-60 minutes each
- Used Mom Test principles (past behavior, not hypotheticals)
- No leading questions about the solution
- Pain scores self-reported by interviewees
- Purchase intent asked after describing solution concept
